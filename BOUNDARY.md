# BOUNDARY

## Exact Demonstrator Boundary, Simulation Law, and Claim Discipline Rule

This document defines the exact public boundary of the WattsProtect™ Demonstrator repository.

It exists so that no reader, reviewer, observer, institution, engineer, developer, diligence party, or third party may enlarge the repository’s meaning beyond what is actually being shown and actually being claimed.

The governing rule is exact:

No claim may exceed implemented, tested, validated, and released reality.

If anything in this repository appears broader than that rule permits, the narrower and more truthful reading controls.
