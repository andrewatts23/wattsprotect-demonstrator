Mission

What this demonstrator exists to prove

This demonstrator exists to prove, in review-safe and rights-preserving form, that WattsProtect™ is a distinct architecture class: a system in which contextual change can become governed calibration risk, human action remains attributable, and event meaning survives through evidence-preserving audit structure.

  • Contextual and environmental signals may influence forward-looking risk interpretation.
  • Predictive outputs remain bounded, non-autonomous, and reviewable.
  • Governed workflow begins through explicit event progression rather than casual alerting.
  • Material action remains attributable to an authorized actor.
  • Evidence completion is required before closure may occur.
  • Audit meaning remains reconstructible after the event path concludes.
